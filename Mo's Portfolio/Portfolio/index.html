ABOUT ME

Hi there, I’m Mohamed – A front-end developer with a keen interest in user-centred design. My earliest exposure to the world of web development was in school – exploring a software where I created a cool platform game for my classmates and I. To the dismay of my teachers, I’d play this game during class and think of ways I could create new levels or develop new features. Looking back at this childhood memory, I realised technology has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember and that I wanted to contribute something to the future of our tech world.

Initially, this passion drove me to work in the field of mechanical engineering, where I was able to create physical parts for planes and automotive vehicles. I greatly enjoyed the process of guiding projects from initial ideation to the final product. I developed skills in iterative design, problem-solving and attention to detail. Over the last year, I have transferred these skills into software development, where I have been creating websites and working on projects for clients. I have trained myself in writing clean HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript and have proficiently used these programmes across multiple projects. Through completing courses, I have learned how to use RESTful APIs, Node.js, Express and npm packages and apply them to websites. My hunger for knowledge and determination to progress makes me confident that I can contribute greatly to the developer world.
